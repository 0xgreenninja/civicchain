A Multi-Stakeholder Ecosystem

Core Stewards

Team members proposing initiatives, reporting progress, and coordinating execution across the region.

Local leaders and independent validators who verify project milestones and real-world impact.

Donors and volunteers participating in signaling priorities and delegating votes for long-term growth.

Decisions & Fund Management

Funds are managed through on-chain multisig wallets and smart contract grants. Voting power is earned through reputation and community validation.
