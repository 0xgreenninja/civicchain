A Multi-Stakeholder System

Core Stewards

Team members responsible for proposing initiatives, reporting progress, and coordinating execution.

Community Validators

Local leaders and independent validators who verify project milestones and real-world impact.

ReFi Allies

Web3 projects, climate protocols, and foundations that contribute funding, tools, and expertise.

Public Supporters

Donors and advocates who participate in discussions and signal strategic impact priorities.

Decisions & Fund Management

Funds are managed through on-chain multisig wallets and smart contract grants. Votes cover project approvals, grant allocations, and milestone releases.

"If impact cannot be verified through geo-tagged media and community attestations, funds remain locked."
